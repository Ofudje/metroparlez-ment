Our Story Matters

Metro Parlez-Ment was built on a simple belief that communities thrive when people care for one another

Who We Are

Metro Parlez-Ment, also known as Port Harcourt Parliament, began over 20 years ago when a group of young professionals came together to discuss current affairs and build meaningful relationships. Inspired by a shared desire to make a positive impact, the group transitioned into organized charitable work.

In September 2007, the organization was officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) as a non-ethnic, non-religious, and non-political social and philanthropic organization with 30 founding members. Guided by a registered Board of Trustees, Metro Parlez-Ment is committed to improving lives through consistent community service.

Over the years, the organization has expanded its charitable initiatives to include medical support for indigent patients, assistance to orphanages and charity homes, educational sponsorships, school equipment donations, correctional centre rehabilitation, and collaborative community outreach programs, including partnerships supporting widows.

Today, Metro Parlez-Ment has grown to 58 members, including diaspora members in Lagos, Canada, the United Kingdom, and the United States, who actively participate in the organization's activities virtually. The organization remains dedicated to its mission of carrying out charity with a pure heart and creating lasting positive impact in communities across Nigeria.

Our Mission & Vision

To become a leading humanitarian and social-impact club in Port Harcourt and beyond, distinguished by integrity, unity, compassionate service and recognized as a symbol of hope for the less privileged.

Our Core Values

These five pillars guide every decision we make and every action we take in service to our community.

Integrity

We uphold honorable conduct, transparency and accountability in all our actions, protecting the image and dignity of the Club at all times.

Unity

We reject ethnic, political, and religious division, promoting harmony, mutual respect, and brotherhood among members and within the wider community.

Charity

We are committed to uplifting the less privileged through consistent acts of kindness, generosity, and meaningful humanitarian interventions.

Compassion

We act with empathy and love, responding to the needs of the needy, the distressed and the vulnerable with sincerity and care.

Service

We believe leadership is service. We dedicate our time, resources and talent to improving lives and strengthening our community.
