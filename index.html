About Us

Metro Parlez-ment, also known as Port Harcourt Parliament, began over 20 years ago as a gathering of young professionals committed to meaningful dialogue and community impact.

In September 2007, the club was officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) as a non-ethnic, non-religious, and non-political social and philanthropic organization.

Metro Parlez-ment is more than just a humanitarian organization we are a community of compassionate individuals committed to making a tangible difference in the lives of vulnerable populations.

Focus Areas

We concentrate our efforts on four key areas to maximize our impact and create sustainable change in communities.

Orphanage Home Support

Providing essential supplies, educational materials, and emotional support to orphanages, ensuring every child has access to basic necessities and a nurturing environment.

Learn More

Medical Outreach Programs

Organizing medical outreach programs that provide free health checks, medications, and healthcare support to vulnerable communities, ensuring people receive the care they need to live healthy lives.

Learn More

School Support

Providing educational materials, scholarships, and learning resources to schools in underserved communities. Our goal is to empower children through education and create opportunities for a brighter future.

Learn More

Compassion Home Support

Supporting compassion homes by providing essential supplies, emotional care, and welfare support to children and individuals in need, helping create a safe and nurturing environment.
